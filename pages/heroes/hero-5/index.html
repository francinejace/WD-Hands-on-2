Itsuki Nakano


Itsuki Nakano is the fifth sister of the Nakano Quintuplets. She is attributed as the "first girl" of the series. Itsuki is serious and diligent, yet she's also a glutton and crybaby. Itsuki is a serious girl, usually getting up early compared to her sisters. She also has good manners, to the point where she cares a lot about manners. She has never been late or skipped a class, and always speaks polite language.
